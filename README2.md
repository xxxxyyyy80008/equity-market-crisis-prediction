This project implements **Gradient Boosted Decision Trees (GBDT)** to predict equity market crisis regimes. *   **Feature Engineering:** Includes volatility indices, moving averages, and momentum indicators.
*   **Robust Evaluation:** Backtesting framework with precision-recall analysis for rare "crisis" events.
